Hello, I'm Ally Hernandez!

I'm a second year design major and advertising minor at the University of San Francisco. I was born and raised in South SF and I've always loved going out into the city to gather inspiration for my work. While I do design in my free time, I also have taken art classes at my high school Sacred Heart Cathedral which is located in the heart of the city and at ArtWorks Fine Studio located in West Portal. I've worked with a variety of art mediums ranging from traditional (oil pastels, oil painting, acrylic painting, color pencils, watercolor, charcoal, etc) to digital (photography, digital drawing/illustration, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop). Most of my work has been illustration based, from my AP Studio Art portfolio to fun little personal projects like fanart.

I am currently focusing more on my digital work as I pursue my bachelor's degree at USF and I've been experimenting with HTML and CSS coding. I'm very enthusiastic about taking on new design and art challenges to further improve my skill set! I'm interested in knowing more about different kinds of design such as industrial design, graphic design, character design, UX/UI design and more.
